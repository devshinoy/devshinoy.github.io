What is TED?

TED is a non profit devoted to "ideas worth spreading". Started as a four-day conference in California 30 years ago, TED has grown to support its mission with multiple initiatives. The two annual TED Conferences invite the world's leading thinkers and doers to speak for 18 minutes or less. Many of these talks are then made available, free, at TED.com.

What is TEDx?

TEDx event are locally organized TED style events. Here 'x' stands for "Independently organized TED event". At a TEDx event, TED talk videos and live speakers combine to spark deep discussion and connection. The TED Conference provides general guidance for the TEDx program, but individual TEDx events are self-organized(Subject to certain rules and regulations). Our event is called TEDxMITS.

Got more questions?

  • Why should I attend a TEDx event?

    TEDx is an event where a set of carefully curated speakers will talk about their one great idea that can impact the local community. When you attend such an event, you get to hear lot of ideas that will inspire as well as widen your thinking. Every TEDx event is carefully curated for best experience for the audience according to certain global guidelines hence unmatched by similar events. Attending a TEDx event help you have an experience that watching talks online can not provide, besides it gives an opportunity to meet like minded people and network with them & speakers as well.

  • How can I attend a TEDx event?

    Hundreds of TEDx events happen every year. A list of all TEDx event can be found here

  • What can I expect from TEDxMITS?

    A day of engaging speakers dedicated to ideas worth spreading. The program will feature over 5 amazingly talented people over a half-day. In the spirit of TEDx, speakers will perform short, unique & interactive lectures created exclusively for TEDxMITS.
