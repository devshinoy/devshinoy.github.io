Don't Build a Product, Build an Experience
John Mathew

What makes Taj Mahal Special? Would building an exact replica make the replica as special as the Taj? In this fascinating talk, John Mathew give insights on importance of building an experience and not just a product.
John is the CEO and Co-Founder of Riafy Technologies, one of the premier app studios in the world.
How Theory transformed Carnatic Music
Aalok Thakkar

With a keen eye for details, Aalok Thakkar brings out the simplicity and sophistication of the world around us. In this talk he unravel the patterns in Carnatic ragas using a mathematical spirit of inquiry. Join him in this talk about the history and formalization of Carnatic music!
Easy Greening: How Small Steps in Daily Life Can Make a Difference
Aardra Chandra Mouli

We all know that small steps can lead to big differences. In this interesting talk, Ms. Aardra Chandra Mouli discusses about the small steps we individuals can take to participate in the re-greening of Earth.
Aardra is the co-founder of Aeka Biochemicals Pvt. Ltd, the first fully women-owned biotech and biochemical start-up venture in Kerala, India.
An 8 Year Olds Journey from Home to Largest Makerfest in the World
Saarang Sumesh

"I am a maker." These are the words of Saarang Sumesh, a seven year old innovator from Kerala, India. Saarang started making complex models like internal combustion engine when he was just three years old and was the Youngest exhibitor at the largest Maker Faire in the world at Silicon Valley, USA. Watch this talk to get to know his amazing story.
Is Kochi 'Water' Safe?
Dr. Sunny George

Water is the elixir of life on Earth. But as Dr. Sunny George, a limnologist and Director of SCMS Water Institute suggests in this interesting talk, it also posses a safety challenge!. He discusses about Urban Water Safety and shares tips on how local authorities can save and preserve water.
